Part [02]

Our Services

Data Management & Sharing Plans

Review DMSPs for NIH, NSF, NEH, and DOE grants. Help meet funder and journal requirements.

Data Management Practices

Advise on data, image, code, and file management. Assist with documentation and naming conventions.

Reproducibility

Best practices for creating/sharing code, GitHub workflows, and statistical methods.

Data Curation & Repositories

Support researchers depositing data into URRR and finding appropriate repositories.

Data Visualization

Training and advice on creating and sharing visualizations with various tools.

Data Tools

Assist with LabArchives, OSF, protocols.io, DMPTool, ICPSR, and BioRender.
